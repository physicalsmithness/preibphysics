New I-V Circuit Components

1. Variable Power Supply / Variable Battery

Standard multiple-cell battery symbol struck by a precise 45-degree arrow.

2. I-V Characteristic Circuit (Variable Power Supply)

Uses the variable battery at the top to change the potential difference. Ammeter in series, Voltmeter parallel across the fixed resistor.

3. I-V Characteristic Circuit (Variable Resistor)

Uses a standard battery, with a variable resistor situated on the right side of the series loop to act as a variable voltage divider.
